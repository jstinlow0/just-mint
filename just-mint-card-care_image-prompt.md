## SHORT VERSION (for AI image generators)

A photograph of a single A4 portrait pricing sign for a trading-card cleaning atelier called **Just Mint Card Care**. The sign feels like a page from a nineteenth-century apothecary's formulary or the catalogue card of a small museum — quiet, restrained, master-crafted, conservation-grade.

The composition is anchored by a tall two-line wordmark in the upper-left set in a high-contrast didone serif (Italiana-style — hairline-thin verticals, sharp brackets, refined curves) reading **"Just Mint"** above **"Card Care"** in deep forest green. To its upper-right floats a small circular monogram seal — two concentric thin forest-green rings around the letters **"JMCC"** in a precise mono typeface. A single hairline rule in deep forest green divides the header from the body.

Below, a small uppercase label in tracked Work Sans reads **"Card Care Services"** with a delicate hairline rule and the annotation *"i · ii · iii · iv"* in mint-deep monospace at the right end. Four catalogue rows follow, each a small monograph: a Roman numeral in mint-deep monospace on the left; the service name in IBM-Plex-Serif-style small caps; a one-sentence italic description in a refined book-serif italic; and on the right, a turnaround time in monospace above a large monospace price. Hairline rules separate the rows.

Listed in order:

- **II. Dents** — *Controlled relaxation and re-bonding of surface layers. Clean + Polish included.* — 1 week — **$50**
- **III. Corner · Edge Lifts** — *Controlled relaxation and re-bonding of surface layers. Clean + Polish included.* — 1–2 weeks — **$30**

A fifth row, set slightly smaller and tinted muted bronze, marked with an em-dash instead of a numeral, reads **"Bulk Submissions"** with the italic line *"A standing courtesy on larger consignments — please ask when scheduling."* and the price **"10% off."**

A heavier forest hairline divides the catalogue from the lower half, which splits into two columns separated by a faint vertical mint rule.

The **left column** is labeled **"Payment"** and holds two square QR codes side by side, each framed in a thin forest border with delicate mint corner-bracket register marks at the four outer corners — like the registration crops on a printer's proof. Beneath each QR sits a small uppercase label and a monospace handle: **"Venmo · @Justin-Lo-18"** and **"PayPal · Justin Lo."** Below the row, centered in italic serif: *"Cash also accepted."*

The **right column** is labeled **"Connect"** and holds a single, slightly larger square QR code in the same corner-bracketed frame, captioned **"Instagram · @justmintcardcare."** Below that, a centered monospace email line reads **"justminttcg@gmail.com"**, and beneath it an italic line: *"DM to schedule · Local drop-off."*

At the foot of the page, two short italic notes sit side by side, each prefixed with a tiny pale-mint chip reading **"NOTE"** in tracked uppercase: *"Whitening cannot be fixed."* and *"Ask about bundle deals for cleaning."*

The overall palette is held to four notes: warm cream paper, frosted-glass mint accents, deep forest green ink, and a single thread of muted bronze. Nothing is bright; nothing is saturated. The composition reads with the spatial logic of a watchmaker's tray — generous breathing room, exact margins, hairlines at copperplate weight, every interval tuned by a master typesetter. There is no tagline, no emoji, no decorative flourish. The page is a quiet, museum-grade artifact.

## STYLE NOTES (universal)

- Photographed, not illustrated. Real paper, real ink.
- Color grade: muted, slightly warm, low-saturation. Earthy mint and cream dominate.
- Lens: 50mm or 85mm equivalent, no fisheye or wide-angle distortion.
- Lighting: soft, diffuse, indirect daylight. No harsh studio flash, no neon.
- Mood: quiet, considered, conservation-grade. The viewer should sense the sign was made by someone at the very top of their craft.
- Definitely **not**: clip-art aesthetic, cartoonish illustration, neon greens, glossy gradients, drop shadows, faux 3D, marketing-flyer typography.
